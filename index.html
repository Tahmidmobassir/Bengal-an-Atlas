আমার সোনার বাংলা, আমি তোমায় ভালোবাসি

Tagore wrote that line in 1905, in protest against a partition Curzon would announce that October. Sixty-six years later it became the national anthem of a country that did not yet exist when he wrote it.

Golden Bengal, I love you. The history of the Bengali people is the history of writing that line, in two scripts, on either side of a border that water keeps trying to dissolve, in the teeth of every reason not to.
